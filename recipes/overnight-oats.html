Overnight Oats

Description

Overnight oats are an easy and healthy breakfast option. It takes about 5-10 mins to make and prep so that you can grab and go in the morning. They are good in the fridge for up to 1 week, so you can make a few a at a time.

Ingredients

  • Milk - 2% milk, oat milk, almond milk, or other alternative.
  • Greek yogurt
  • Oats
  • Honey
  • Chia Seeds
  • Cinnamon
  • Berries

Steps

  1. Combine all the ingredients besides the fruit in a jar, seal, and shake. Or just mix with a spoon.
  2. Open the jar and put the fruits in.
  3. Seal the jar and refrigerate overnight.
